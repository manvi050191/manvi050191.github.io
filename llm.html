What are Large Language Models?

Large Language Models (LLMs) are AI models trained on vast amounts of text data to understand and generate human-like text. Examples include OpenAI’s GPT, Google’s BERT, and Meta’s LLaMA.

Key Features of LLMs

  • Natural Language Processing (NLP) capabilities
  • Ability to generate coherent and contextual responses
  • Pre-trained on massive datasets and fine-tuned for specific tasks
  • Applications in chatbots, content generation, coding assistance, and more

Applications of LLMs

  • Chatbots & Virtual Assistants
  • Text summarization & translation
  • Code generation & debugging
  • Medical & legal document analysis
